How It Works

The Reynolds number Re = UL/ν controls whether flow is smooth or chaotic. Low Re: viscosity dominates (laminar). High Re: inertia dominates (turbulent).

Under the hood, this uses the Lattice Boltzmann Method — instead of solving fluid equations directly, it simulates particles streaming and colliding on a grid. Each cell tracks 9 velocity directions. At every step: stream (shift to neighbors), collide (relax toward equilibrium), bounce-back (reflect off walls).
